MuleShoe Ranch is a cow calf operation originally founded in the 1920's. The ranch is located in the beautiful Sacramento Mountains of New Mexico, on 360 acres, adjacent to the historic Camp Mary White. The ranch features an apple orchard, pond with trout and bass, main house, and several small outbuildings. The ranch has recently added horses and goats to the operation.
